ClickTale records on page activity at a very deep level: their javascript records mouse movements and clicks to generate easy to understand heatmaps and videos.
ClickTale helps you understand not only what happens on your site, but why: seeing where your users clicked before leaving is an important part of the optimization journey.
ClickTale also offers form analytics, to improve on one of the most critical parts towards conversions.
Enterprise packages come with other exciting features, like advanced funnels - isolate videos from people who left at a specific step - and segmentation.
