An interesting talk from Joshua Bixby, Strangeloop President, on Mobile Performance and its relationship with business KPIs. Bixby's speech is both interesting for its contents and the clear and engaging information visualization.

After providing context about how the mobile landscape changed in the last 3 years, Bixby details the outcomes of a Strangeloop experiment that measured how bounce rate and conversion values change at different network speeds and delays.
