Everyone is talking about wearable devices, but in most cases it's hard to find a real value in those products. Nymi could change that. It's a simple idea that solves one of the fundamental problems about the internet: password based authentication. Let your heart do the work for you.

While I think it's difficult for nymi to succeed on a broad market, I expect big players like Apple and Google to go in this direction. Providing biometric authentication at the OS level is key to truly eradicate passwords.

This is exactly what should be at the core of an iWatch: a system that knows exactly who you are and securely shares that information with your smartphone.
