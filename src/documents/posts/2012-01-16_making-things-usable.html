Professor Jan Borchers from Aachen University summarizes 9 principles to remember every time we design a product, be it a physical object or a digital one.

  1. Simplicity
  2. Gestalt Laws
  3. Visibility and Feedback
  4. Natural Mappings
  5. The Principle of Least Surprise
  6. Dialog, not Monologue
  7. Error Tolerance
  8. Vertical Design
  9. Aestethics

If you like this TEDx talk, be sure to also check out on iTunes U, because there is more from professor Borchers: a good starting point is the Designing Interactive Systems course.
